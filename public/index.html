Kantratam

(cont - tra - tom)

Kantratam is a hard rock band from Nashville, TN named for the classical ballet term Contretemps, meaning “beating against time.” Other definitions include : "an unexpected and unfortunate occurrence" or : "a minor dispute or disagreement". We rehearse regularly at your mom's house. She likes it.

Adele Pomerenke

Vocals

Adele Marie Pomerenke aka AMP is an immensely skilled vocalist, multi-instrumentalist, songwriter, actor, model, ballerina, and probably a bunch of other stuff too. A native of Selah, Washington, she originally moved to Tennessee to dance with the Nashville Ballet. She can kill a man with a single kick to the spine, so don't let her kick you in the spine.

Harold King

Drums

A lifelong resident of Murfreesboro, Tennessee, Harold King is thought by some to be one of the greatest drummers of all time. He also enjoys a nice suit.

Mike Pomerenke

Bass

Hailing from Yakima, Washington, Mike Pomerenke can fix anything that breaks, build his own instruments, and grow a full beard in 45 minutes. His greatest skill however, is his ability to melt your face with a bass.

Chris Duke

Guitar

Christopher Daniel Duke was born in Murfreesboro, Tennessee and grew up in Smithville, Tennessee, or more accurately, rural unincorporated DeKalb County, where he was homeschooled. He is primarily self-taught and is the former guitarist of The Cosmic Americana Band and former frontman of Tadpole Jar. His super power is mild Autism. Mike owes him money.
